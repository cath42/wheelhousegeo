Meet Our Founder

Leadership built on expertise, innovation, and results

David Williamson

Founder & Principal Consultant

David Williamson brings decades of experience in geoscience consulting, combining deep technical expertise with innovative problem-solving approaches. As the founder of Wheelhouse Geoscience, he has built a reputation for delivering exceptional results across diverse projects in the energy sector and beyond.

Industry Leadership

Recognized expert in geosteering with pioneering work in 'retroactive batch geosteering' techniques.

Educational Excellence

Develops tailored geosteering courses and comprehensive online training programs.

Proven Track Record

Led over 90 technical evaluations demonstrating consistent high-quality delivery.

Energy Transition

Forward-thinking approach to CCS, renewable natural gas, and lithium extraction.

Leadership Philosophy

"Success in geoscience consulting comes from combining deep technical knowledge with innovative thinking and collaborative partnerships. Every project presents unique challenges that require tailored solutions, and our role is to bring the right expertise to bear at the right time."

David's approach to leadership centers on building strong relationships with clients and consortium partners, fostering an environment where technical excellence and creative problem-solving can thrive. His commitment to continuous learning and industry advancement ensures that Wheelhouse Geoscience remains at the forefront of geoscience innovation.

Vision for the Future

Under David's leadership, Wheelhouse Geoscience continues to evolve and adapt to meet the changing needs of the energy industry. From traditional oil and gas consulting to emerging opportunities in renewable energy and environmental solutions, the company's vision encompasses a comprehensive approach to geoscience challenges.

The development of educational programs and online courses reflects David's commitment to advancing industry knowledge and sharing expertise with the next generation of geoscience professionals. This dedication to knowledge transfer ensures that best practices and innovative techniques continue to benefit the broader geoscience community.
