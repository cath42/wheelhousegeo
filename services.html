What We Don't Do…And Never Will

We do not over-promise and under-deliver.

Being on the same page about expectations is crucial: We make no assumptions and ask questions (and are always open to yours). Being a generalist gets someone only so far; we pull in specialists-by-discipline as needed to tackle the tough stuff. We charge hourly rather than by the day or the project, because we want the work to dictate what "completion" looks like, not the clock.

We do not bite off more than we can chew.

We are a boutique consultancy. We know we're at our best when we provide a lot of service to a few clients rather than providing light service to a lot of clients. If we do not have the bandwidth to tackle your needs, we are honest about it. Time is money, and we won't waste yours.

We do not abuse our client's hard-earned trust.

No price gouging: Our rates are fair, and our invoices are transparent. Our client's data, activities, and plans are kept confidential. Our clients are entitled not only to the final product but also to the data and analytics behind it. We make recommendations thoughtfully, just as if it was our company and our money on the line.
