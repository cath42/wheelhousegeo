Our Story

Wheelhouse Geoscience was founded on the principle that exceptional results come from bringing together the right combination of expertise, experience, and innovative thinking. Our consortium model allows us to deploy exactly the right skills for each unique project, whether working individually or as integrated teams.

From our earliest days, we've focused on building long-term relationships with our clients, understanding that trust is earned through consistent delivery of high-quality work and reliable expertise. Our track record of over 90 technical evaluations since inception demonstrates our commitment to excellence across diverse project types.

As the industry evolves, so do we. We've expanded our capabilities to include energy transition projects, embracing new challenges in carbon capture & sequestration, renewable natural gas, and lithium extraction while maintaining our core strengths in traditional energy sector consulting.

Our Philosophy

Just as a ship's wheelhouse contains everything needed to navigate challenging waters, Wheelhouse Geoscience brings together all the expertise, technology, and experience necessary to guide your projects to successful completion. We are your command center for geoscience solutions.
